* [BPMN method and style](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Bpmn-Method-Style-Implementers-Guide/dp/0982368119/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1531487366&sr=8-1&keywords=bpmn+method+and+style)
